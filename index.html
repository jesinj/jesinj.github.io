Dr. Jesin James

Senior Lecturer, University of Auckland

About

I am a Senior Lecturer in the Faculty of Engineering and Design at the University of Auckland. My research focuses on speech signal processing, machine learning, under-resourced languages, and engineering education. [1](https://profiles.auckland.ac.nz/jesin-james)

My work has included the development of text-to-speech systems, including for Malayalam, and research on empathetic speech for healthcare robots, aiming to improve naturalness and user acceptance. [1](https://profiles.auckland.ac.nz/jesin-james)

I completed my PhD at the University of Auckland, where I worked on speech technologies in the context of human–robot interaction. [1](https://profiles.auckland.ac.nz/jesin-james)
